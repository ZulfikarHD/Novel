- Displays brilliant engineering mind and competitive spirit

**Elara Frost:**
- Observant and perceptive, notices Luna's condition

**Zain Ember:**
- Enthusiastic and energetic with wild red hair
- Suggests unorthodox ideas (spinning arrays) that prove valuable
- Works at uncle's Harmonic Foundries, can source materials
- Provides cover for Luna's rest periods with his tangents

- Esnar has 36-hour days

**Mood:** Morning energy transitioning to classroom collaboration, underlying tension about Luna's visible deterioration balanced by teamwork excitement
