# Luna Archi — Timeline

## Birth - Age 8 — The Illness Years
**Key Events:**
- Born into family of minor magic crafters with rare magic weakness
- Unstable magic reserves require constant healer intervention
- Parents (Iruma and Erwin Archi) often absent for work
- Frequent nighttime illness flare-ups become defining moments
- Vixia establishes pattern: sits by her bedside, quiet presence, gentle humming
- Those nights shape her understanding of love as presence, not words

**Psychological Formation:**
- Learns vulnerability means being alone (except for Vixia)
- Showing weakness begins to feel dangerous
- Develops early coping mechanism: perform strength to feel safe
- Fear of abandonment takes root (unconscious)
- Vixia becomes emotional anchor and lifeline

**Impact:**
- Creates unbreakable bond with Vixia
- Associates care with silent presence, not words
- Begins internalizing: "I must be strong to be safe"

---

## Age 8-11 — Awakening Resolve & Building Armor
**Key Developments:**
- Finally stands on her own, enters world unkind to weakness
- Whispers follow her (pale blue nightgowns, black gloves hiding bruises)
- Begins constructing confident persona as protection
- Discovers talent for magical engineering
- Pours frustration into magic blueprints from academy archives
- Starts emulating mother's work (aspiration + proving worth)
- Develops competitive dynamic with Vixia (connection through challenge)

**Behavioral Patterns Emerge:**
- Teasing as love language
- "Race you" instead of "stay with me"
- Verbal rejection → quiet implementation (pride vs. logic)
- Social charm that maintains emotional distance
- Cannot ask for help directly

**Skills Acquired:**
- Basic magical engineering knowledge
- Social navigation and performance
- Emotional compartmentalization
- Defense mechanisms (barely aware of them)

**Impact:**
- Performance becomes partly genuine, partly protective
- Engineering passion mixed with need to prove she's not fragile
- Creates public persona: confident, playful, approachable
- Maintains private reality: guarded, lonely except for Vixia

---

## Age 11 — Engineering Breakthrough
**Key Achievement:**
- Creates first Mana Conduit Glove (turns faint life energy into steady sparks)
- Recognition as prodigy begins among peers
- Pre-dawn work habits establish
- Fellow students call her "odd" but acknowledge genius

**Vixia's Protective Moments Intensify:**
- Workshop incident: three older students corner Luna, insult her health/projects
- Vixia steps between her and bullies, quiet authority makes them back down
- He begins secretly stopping rumors, showing headmistress proof of wrongdoing
- Luna catches him once—slipping note to teacher in shadowed corridor
- Never speaks of it, but fierce loyalty deepens

**Impact:**
- Engineering becomes central to identity
- Success reinforces: "If I'm brilliant, I'm safe"
- Devotion to Vixia intensifies (he protects without asking for recognition)

---

## Age 11-16 — Deepening Mastery & Partial Healing
**Key Developments:**
- Advances engineering skills dramatically
- Develops crystal amplifiers, special coatings, complex circuits
- Works on life-force stabilizer (personal + practical motivation)
- Supports Vixia's Resonance studies (fixes equipment, improves arrays)
- Plans Unknown Region expedition together

**Emotional Evolution:**
- Armor becomes integrated—partly genuine confidence now
- Begins recognizing own patterns (self-awareness increases)
- Learns to be selectively vulnerable with Vixia
- Can express gratitude (with grumbling)
- Physical affection becomes natural love language

**Relationship Deepening:**
- Vixia's emotional anchor role: matcha tea, chocolate cake, steady hand on shoulder
- Small insights from his magic studies at perfect moments
- Luna treasures these more than any award
- Ever since: matcha and chocolate become favorite tastes
- Playful teasing—gentle pinches, surprise hugs at dawn—solidify

**But Patterns Remain:**
- "I'm fine" reflex defaults (requires pushing through)
- Cannot say "you were right" immediately (implements first, grumbles later)
- Won't ask for help before trying herself
- Cannot say "I need you" directly (shows through actions)
- Tests boundaries occasionally (old fear: will he stay?)

**Social Life:**
- Popular but lonely (many acquaintances, few real friends)
- Particularly uncomfortable with male romantic attention
- Maintains friendly distance from peers
- Only Vixia sees genuine vulnerability

---

## Age 16 — Current State
**Present Circumstances:**
- Recognized engineering prodigy, preparing for Unknown Region
- Has Mana Conduit Glove, working on life-force stabilizer
- Supports Vixia's dragon Resonance dream
- Plans to find rare magic stones for inventions
- Chronic illness managed but present (sometimes background, sometimes debilitating)

**Who She Is Now:**
- Genuinely confident (not just performing, though reflexes remain)
- Warm and playful (real affection with Vixia, guarded with others)
- Brilliant and driven (passion + unconscious need to prove worth)
- Devoted sister (physical affection, protective loyalty)
- Self-aware (knows patterns, can't fully control them)

**Current Challenges:**
- Pride prevents immediate admission of being wrong
- "I'm fine" defaults even when not fine
- Cannot ask for help directly
- Engineering success partly about proving she's not fragile
- Abandonment fear (quiet, managed, but present)
- Exhausting social performance with non-family

**Current Strengths:**
- Genuine engineering brilliance
- Mostly healthy relationship with Vixia (open enough, guarded enough)
- Resilience in face of ongoing illness
- Self-awareness about psychological patterns
- Real warmth and affection (not purely defensive)

**The Integration:**
- Fragile girl and confident engineer coexist
- Performance and authenticity blur together
- With Vixia: balance between openness and safety
- Not fully healed, but functional and mostly happy
